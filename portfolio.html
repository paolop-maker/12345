Paolo Tanglao

Hi! My name is Paolo Tanglao, and I’m from Salapungan, San Nicolas, San Fernando, Pampanga. I’m 16 years old
and currently a Senior High School student at Pampanga High School. This is my fifth year in high school,
and while most of those years have been spent focusing on my studies and exploring different interests, this year feels special
because it’s my very first year learning how to code. I’ve always been curious about how websites, apps, and
programs work behind the scenes. At first glance, coding seemed intimidating, just a wall of strange symbols and words. But as
I started learning step-by-step, I discovered that it’s actually like solving puzzles and building something entirely from scratch.
It can be challenging, but that’s exactly what makes it exciting. My goal is to grow my skills from creating simple pages to developing
more advanced projects. More than just learning the technical side, I want to use coding to build things that are useful, creative, and
inspiring for others.This is just the start of my journey, and I’m ready to embrace the challenges ahead and see how far I can go.
